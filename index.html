Asian movie directors worth your attention!

Chole Zhao winning Oscar

'Nomadland' director Chole Zhao made history as the first Asian woman to walk home with two Oscars, one for Best Director and one for Best Picture. Her great performance is a great achievement for Asian movie directors. Asian men and women are frequently overlooked for their talent within the film industry. This is especially unfortunate as there are incredible films created by Asian directors. I will introduce Asian movie directors from China, Japan and Korean. These movie directors make contribute to Asian movies and inspire the young in Asia.
